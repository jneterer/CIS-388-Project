What is it that we do?

It is my job to make your lives simpler. Have you ever lent a book to someone and never got it back? Was it because you forgot who you gave it to, what the book was, when you gave it to them? I know I have. Bookshelf offers many awesome features, perfect for your very own at-home "libraries." We allow you to have a record of all of your books you own, where each book has a status indicating whether it is "checked out" (currently being loaned) or "checked in" (on your shelf). When a book is checked out, we help you track who has it, when it was loaned, and the individuals contact information along with some comments about the loan. In addition, we let you log notes on the books and keep track of quotes you want to save while reading.
